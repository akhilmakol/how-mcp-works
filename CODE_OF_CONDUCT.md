# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free environment for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume positive intent while giving clear feedback
- welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and outcomes rather than people

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- personal attacks
- deliberate intimidation
- repeated bad-faith disruption of collaboration

## Reporting

If you experience or observe behavior that violates this code, please report it privately to the project maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and participation that violate this code of conduct.
